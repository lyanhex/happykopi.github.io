About Us

At HAPPY KOPI, we believe that happiness starts with a great cup of coffee. Founded with the dream of spreading joy one brew at a time, we created a space where everyone can feel welcome, relaxed, and recharged. Our coffee is brewed with love, using quality beans and a whole lot of heart.
